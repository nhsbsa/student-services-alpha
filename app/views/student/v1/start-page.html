NHSBSA Student Services provides funding for eligible healthcare, medical, dental or social work students on behalf of the Department of Health and Social Care.

The services we administer are the:

  • NHS Learning Support Fund
  • NHS Bursary
  • Social Work Bursary

Use this service to:

  • find out what funding you're eligible for
  • apply for elements of funding
  • check the status of your applications
  • update your personal details, including your bank account
  • check when your payments are due

You can also manage your personal details on your account. You need to keep these up to date as wrong information could delay your payments.

Before you start

Before creating an account you'll need to tell us your university course details to determine which funding is applicable.

Registering takes around 10 minutes.
