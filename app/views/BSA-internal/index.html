Student Services BSA Internal

Alpha sprint 2 & 3 - Combined system for processors scenario 1

What we created

In total 4 prototype scenarios were available. 2 for processors and 2 for CCS (Contact Centre Staff).

  • (Processor) Start from WFM work item (real example). Go onto a combined system. Find a student, go to their application, making sure they understand that they're in the right funding.
  • (Processor) Start at WFM work item (real example). Go onto a combined system. Find a second student, go to their application, making sure they understand that they're now in SB/SWB.
  • (CCS) Sign into a combined system and search for an LSF student and find their next payment date and information.
  • (CCS) Sign into a combined system and search for an SB student and find their next payment date and information.

What we plan to test

The assumptions we are testing are:

  • Internal users will get confused and make errors if the systems are combined onto one platform
  • Internal users will be confused and unsure of what funding they are on and what the rules are if the system is combined
  • Users will expect all systems if combined to have the same branding
  • A single system will save time & improve ways of working for internal users
